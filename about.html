Our Vision

20+ Years on the Road
Our Story

From Local Fleet to Pan-India Network

Kavya Transports started as a fleet operator in 2004 in Tirunelveli, Tamil Nadu with a vision to revolutionize transportation in South India. What began as a small fleet operation has grown into a comprehensive 3PL logistics provider with a Pan-India network.

Over two decades, we've expanded from TN37 to TN01 to MH10, building strong partnerships across industries. Our diversified service portfolio now includes Road Transportation, Air & Sea Freight, Warehousing Solutions, and Manpower Services.

Our Journey

Milestones on the Highway

From a single route to nationwide coverage - our journey of growth

2004

The Beginning - Fleet Operator

Started operations as a fleet operator in Tirunelveli, Tamil Nadu. The foundation was laid with a commitment to reliable service and customer satisfaction.

2007

Fleet Strength Expansion

Significant expansion of our fleet capacity. Added diverse vehicle types to serve different cargo requirements - from small parcels to heavy freight.

TN37
2011

TN37 - Regional Establishment

Established strong presence in the TN37 (Tirunelveli) region. Built a reputation for dependable transport services in Southern Tamil Nadu.

TN01
2015

TN01 - Chennai Expansion

Major expansion to Chennai (TN01). Opened operations in Tamil Nadu's capital, connecting the southern region to the metropolitan hub.

2017-18

Kavya Travels Launch

Diversified into passenger transportation with the launch of Kavya Travels. Expanded service portfolio to include people movement alongside goods transport.

MH10
2019-20

MH10 - Pan-India Network

Expanded to Maharashtra (MH10) with Mumbai operations. Achieved true Pan-India presence, connecting South to West India with seamless logistics.

Today

Complete 3PL Provider

Full-fledged 3PL logistics provider with Road Transport, Air & Sea Freight, Warehousing, and Manpower Services. Serving Pan-India with offices in Tirunelveli, Coimbatore, Chennai, and Mumbai.
