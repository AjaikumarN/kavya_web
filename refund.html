Refund Policy

Our fair and transparent refund procedures

Home Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

At Kavya Transports, we strive to provide excellent service. However, we understand that circumstances may require cancellations or changes. This policy outlines our refund procedures.

100% Refund

Cancellation more than 48 hours before scheduled pickup

75% Refund

Cancellation 24-48 hours before scheduled pickup

50% Refund

Cancellation less than 24 hours before scheduled pickup

No Refund

After vehicle dispatch or service commencement

1. Cancellation by Customer

1.1 Full Truck Load (FTL) Bookings

Cancellation Time Refund Amount Processing Time
More than 48 hours before pickup 100% of advance paid 5-7 business days
24-48 hours before pickup 75% of advance paid 5-7 business days
Less than 24 hours before pickup 50% of advance paid 5-7 business days
After vehicle dispatch No refund N/A

1.2 Part Truck Load (PTL) Bookings

Cancellation Time Refund Amount Processing Time
Before cargo pickup 100% of amount paid 5-7 business days
After cargo pickup No refund (return charges apply) N/A

2. Cancellation by Kavya Transports

In rare circumstances where we cancel a booking due to:

  • Vehicle breakdown or unavailability
  • Operational constraints
  • Force majeure events

Customers will receive:

  • 100% refund of all amounts paid
  • Priority rebooking at same rates (if applicable)
  • Alternative vehicle arrangement where possible

3. Refund for Service Issues

3.1 Delay Compensation

For guaranteed delivery bookings with delays:

  • Delay up to 6 hours: No compensation
  • Delay 6-12 hours: 10% refund on freight charges
  • Delay 12-24 hours: 20% refund on freight charges
  • Delay over 24 hours: 30% refund on freight charges (maximum)

Note: Delays due to force majeure, customer issues, or external factors are excluded.

3.2 Damage Claims

  • Claims must be filed within 48 hours of delivery
  • Compensation as per declared value or ₹50/kg (whichever is lower)
  • Full replacement value for insured shipments (subject to insurance terms)

4. How to Request a Refund

1

Contact Support

Call our 24/7 helpline at +91 98765 43210 or email refunds@kavyatransports.com

2

Provide Details

Share your booking ID, reason for cancellation, and preferred refund method

3

Verification

Our team will verify the cancellation request and applicable refund amount

4

Refund Processing

Refund will be processed within 5-7 business days to original payment method

5. Refund Methods

Refunds will be processed through the original payment method:

  • Bank Transfer: To the account from which payment was made
  • Credit/Debit Card: Back to the same card (5-10 business days)
  • UPI: To the same UPI ID
  • Cheque: For cash payments, refund by account payee cheque
  • Credit Note: Can be adjusted against future bookings (valid for 6 months)

6. Non-Refundable Items

The following charges are non-refundable:

  • Insurance premium (once policy is issued)
  • Documentation and permit charges (if already processed)
  • Special equipment rental (cranes, forklifts) if already deployed
  • Packaging materials already used
  • Express/priority booking premium (after confirmation)

7. Rescheduling

As an alternative to cancellation, you may reschedule your booking:

  • One free reschedule allowed (up to 24 hours before pickup)
  • Rescheduling within 7 days of original date at no extra charge
  • Rescheduling beyond 7 days may be subject to rate revision
  • Multiple reschedules may incur administrative charges

8. Disputes

If you disagree with a refund decision:

  1. Submit a written appeal to escalations@kavyatransports.com within 7 days
  2. Include booking details and reason for dispute
  3. Our management team will review and respond within 15 business days
  4. Decision of the management team will be final
