01

Road Transportation

Our core service - reliable road freight solutions connecting all major cities across India. With a diverse fleet ranging from TATA Ace to 40ft Trailers, we handle everything from small parcels to heavy bulk cargo with precision and care.

  • Full Truck Load (FTL): Dedicated vehicles for your exclusive cargo
  • Part Truck Load (PTL): Cost-effective shared transport solutions
  • Express Delivery: Time-critical shipments with priority routing
  • ODC (Over Dimensional Cargo): Specialized handling for oversized loads
  • Scheduled Services: Regular routes with guaranteed transit times
  • Door-to-Door: Complete pickup and delivery service Pan-India

Air & Sea Freight

Extend your reach beyond borders with our international freight services. We handle both air and sea cargo with expertise, ensuring your goods reach global destinations safely and efficiently through our network of international partners.

  • Air Freight: Fast international deliveries for time-sensitive cargo
  • Sea Freight (FCL): Full Container Load for large shipments
  • Sea Freight (LCL): Less than Container Load for smaller cargo
  • Customs Clearance: Complete documentation and clearance support
  • Multimodal Transport: Combined air/sea/road solutions
  • Export/Import Services: End-to-end international logistics

Warehousing Solutions
